Following strategic feedback from the Flare Developer Relations team, Savique is evolving beyond a simple "Commitment Vault" into a comprehensive **DeFi Wealth Builder**. These three features shift the protocol from a "Punishment-Only" model to a "Growth-First" ecosystem.

---

## 1. The Yield Engine (Positive Incentive Carrot) ðŸ¥•
**Objective:** Transform locked capital from "idle" to "productive" by integrating with the Flare DeFi ecosystem.

- **Feature:** While a user's funds are locked in a Savique Vault, the protocol automatically supplies that liquidity to top-tier Flare protocols (e.g., **Kinetic** for lending or **Enosys** for liquidity).
- **User Reward:** Users who successfully reach their goals receive **100% of their principal + the accumulated DeFi yield**.
- **The "Stick":** If a user breaks their vault early, they forfeit the **10% penalty** AND **100% of the yield** earned so far to the protocol treasury.
- **Business Model:** Savique takes a small performance fee on the yield of *successful* users, ensuring revenue flows even when users win.

**Objective:** Provide the tools and opportunities for users to save more, fulfilling the "Positive Saving" feedback.

- **Feature:** A dedicated tab within the dashboard serving two purposes:
    - **Saving Sprints:** Curated financial tips tailored for the crypto-native user (e.g., "The 50/30/20 Rule in Stablecoins").
    - **Ecosystem Side-Gigs:** A curated list of "Earn" opportunities within Flare. This could include DAO Bounty tasks, FTSO delegation instructions, or partner protocol testing.
- **Strategic Value:** Turns Savique into a daily-use habit rather than a monthly check-in. It positions the app as a "gateway" to the broader Flare ecosystem.

**Objective:** Replace abstract numbers with a concrete visual representation of the user's future wealth.

- **Impact:** Uses psychological "loss aversion" to prevent early liquidations by visualizing the massive future gain.
